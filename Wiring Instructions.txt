Project	   : Door Lock
Description: This document will provide the step by step hardware
			 wiring instructions in order to set up the door lock.
			 
			 
Hardware: Arduino
	1. Hook up to usb power
	2. Pin 2 to D3 of the RF transmitter
	3. Both wires of the motor to channel B of motor shield

Hardware: Transmitter
	1. Pin D3 of to Pin 2 of Arduino
	2. Ground and power to 5V and Ground provided by the Arduino

Hardware: Lcd
	1. SCL and SDA pins to SCL and SDA pins of the arduino
	2. ground and power to 5V and Ground provided by the Arduino
